The Perfect Crime Scene

Welcome to the interactive guide for "Murder on the Orient Express." This tool is designed to help you explore the mysteries of the famous case solved by Hercule Poirot. The story begins when the luxurious Orient Express train is stopped by a snowstorm in the mountains. This total isolation creates the perfect setting for a crime: a passenger is found dead in his compartment, stabbed a dozen times, with the door locked from the inside.

With the murderer trapped among the passengers, the brilliant Belgian detective Hercule Poirot must unravel a complex web of secrets and lies to find the truth. Navigate through the sections to discover the suspects, examine the clues, and understand the surprising final solution. Use the "Quiz" section to test your knowledge!
